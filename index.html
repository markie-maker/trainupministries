Welcome to TrainUp Ministries

Encourage. Equip. Expose.

In 2017, my thoughtful and considerate boss Kristine, God bless her, sent me on a work-from-home program to avoid wasting time on my five-hour commute. With this practical arrangement, I had enough time to drop off our three children at school and still have lots of time left over. I quickly figured that our kids could do a Bible devotional plan with me with the newfound extra time.
